# Movie Info App

Explore the world of cinema with our Movie Info App, your ultimate companion for discovering everything about movies. Whether you're a cinephile, casual viewer, or simply curious, our app offers comprehensive details on thousands of movies, including:

- **Release Date**: Find out when your favorite movies hit the big screen.
- **Cast and Crew**: Discover the talented actors and directors behind each film.
- **Plot Summary**: Get a quick overview of the storyline.
- **Duration**: Know how long your movie marathon will last.
- **Genres and Ratings**: Explore genres and ratings to find movies that suit your mood.
- **Reviews and Ratings**: Read what others think about the movie.
- **Trailers**: Watch trailers to get a sneak peek before watching.

Whether you're planning a movie night, researching for a project, or simply love movies, our app provides accurate and up-to-date information in a user-friendly interface. Dive into the world of cinema with ease and enhance your movie-watching experience today!

# Tools and Technologies Used:
Our app is built using **Kotlin** for robust backend logic, **Coroutines** for managing asynchronous tasks efficiently, **Jetpack Compose** for creating a modern and responsive UI, and **Firebase** for real-time database management and synchronization.
